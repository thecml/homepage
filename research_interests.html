My research interests are focused around survival analysis, specifically:
  • Competing risks/multi-event: Exploring how to design, implement and evaluate survival models that can predict multiple (sometimes competing) outcomes.
  • Uncertainty estimation: Understanding how to compute, leverage, and communicate uncertainty in deep survival models, e.g., Bayesian and Conformal prediction.
  • Censoring: Understanding censoring mechanisms in survival analysis, including dependent censoring, and developing methods to adjust for these effects.
  • Applications: Investigating how survival analysis can be applied within healthcare (e.g., predicting functional decline in ALS patients) and outside (e.g., predicting remaining useful life in ball bearings).
