• Competing risks/multi-event: Exploring how to design and evaluate survival models when multiple related, but distinct events are present.
  • Applications: Investigating the use of survival analysis and time-to-event methods outside of healthcare, leveraging their unique strengths.
  • Uncertainty estimation: Understanding how to compute, leverage, and communicate uncertainty in deep survival models, e.g., Bayesian and Conformal prediction.
  • Evaluation: Improving evaluation under various kinds of censoring, e.g., dependent censoring, and how to assess survival predictions for individual patients.
