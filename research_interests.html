• Competing risks/multi-event: Exploring how to design, implement and evaluate survival models that can predict multiple (sometimes exclusive) outcomes.
  • Uncertainty estimation: Understanding how to compute, leverage, and communicate uncertainty in deep survival models, e.g., Bayesian and Conformal prediction.
  • Applications: Investigating how survial analysis can be applied within healthcare (e.g., functional decline in ALS patients) and outside (e.g., remaining useful life prediction).
  • Evaluation: Developing new techniques to evaluate models under various kinds of censoring, e.g., dependent censoring, and to assess individual predictions.
